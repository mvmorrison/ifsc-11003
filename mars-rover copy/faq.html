Frequently Asked Questions

What's the main findings of the Curiosity Rover?

Curiosity has discovered a diverse mix of organic molecules on Mars, including chemicals considering building blocks for the origin of life on Earth.

What is the importance of these discoveries?

They are significant for the search in extraterrestrial life in our solar system, since they provide evidence that ancient organic matter is perserved on Mars.

What challenges do researches face in identifying signs of past life?

Researchers cannot distinguish the difference between organic compounds from potential past life and those formed by geologic processes or delivered by meteorites.
