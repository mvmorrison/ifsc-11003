Mission Timeline

Perserverance

Mission Timeline detailed below.

2020

Perserverance launches on July 30th and enters a cruise phase of approximately seven months.

2021

Perseverance successfully lands on February 18th, 2021, and its first sciene campaign began on June 1st, 2021.

2025

As of September 15th, 2025, Perserverance has been operational for 1625 sols (about 1670 Earth days) and is continues to collect samples and conduct scientific research on Mars to this day.

Curiosity

Mission Timeline detailed below.

2011

Curiosity launches on November 26th, 2011.

2012

Curiosity successfully lands on August 6th, 2012 in Gale Crater, beginning its mission.

2025

The Curiosity Rover has been in Gale Crater for 4664 sols (4,792 Earth days) and it continues to explore and gather data on Mars.
